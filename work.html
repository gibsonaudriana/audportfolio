Work: Pages

Read all about it! As a converted journalism major, my main interests center around news design and the importance of an eye grasping layout. In most ways, the information presented is just as important as its presentation. Here is my work designed using a combination of Indesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator (when I'm feeling fancy).
